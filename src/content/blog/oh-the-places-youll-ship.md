The world’s moving fast so it's time to keep up   
You’re on Spring Break now so it’s time to go nuts!  
On a boat, in a car, while you’re flying by plane  
The places you ship will not be quiet the same  

From the Clouds to the Cloud in a Docker container  
With a phone in your pocket you're your own maintainer  

But the site has to be mobile first  
Claude, Make no mistake!  
Fix a line while in a line  
“Dad, how long will this take?”  

Pick a horse on this carousel, chose a webstack  
Bun over yarn, Vite, of course. no more webpack  
You buckle right up but the ride is now broken  
There’s no time for idle when you’ve got all these tokens!  
  
Solve a race condition in Mario Kart  
The millionth PR thanks to Dario’s smarts  
Burn a small rainforest for the app’s new dark mode  
Rewrite in it Rust cause you're always on the Go  
  
Ship by the pool or on a fake little beach  
Or while posing for pics with a princess named Peach  
Do some hackery, sipping a cold daiquiri  
Could this really be the happiest place?  

“Should I pivot to video?” That’s what you say  
As the rain starts to fall over Mickey’s parade  
Write a shell script while slurping a cold limeade  
But there’s no open claws at this all you can eat buffet  

“Honey, take a break”  
“You're absolutely right!”  
But dark mode is still broken  
And you can’t see the light
